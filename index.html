What is Bo Lip?

Nicotine.
No smoke.
No fuss.

A nicotine pouch is a small, smoke-free, spit-free pouch you place under your upper lip. You get the hit, without the harm of tobacco smoke — and without anyone even noticing. Bo Lip is the proudly South African version of a global movement.
